Main Characters

Katniss Everdeen
Katniss Everdeen is the main protagonist of The Hunger games. She’s a determined and strong willed 16 year old living in the coal mining District 12. Collins describes her as a straight haired girl who has olive coloured skin and light gray eyes.
Peeta Mellark
He is the male tribute of district 12 for the hunger games. Peeta is the baker’s son and works at a bakery where he decorates cake. He is a likable character and selfless character . He has blond hair and bright blue eyes.
Gale Hawthrone
Gale is the best friend of Katniss Everdeen. They met at the age of twelve when hunting in the woods after both of their fathers died in the same mining explosion.

Capitol Characters

Haymitch Abernathy
Haymich is Katniss and Peeta’s mentor in the Hunger Games. He is the only living victor among the two victors that survived the hunger games from district 12.
Effie Trinket
Effie is the escort of the district 12 tributes and also chose the tributes for the hunger games. She is very prim and proper and is known for her Capitol accent. Like all other capitol residents , she dresses very flamboyantly.
Cinna
Cinna is Katniss’s stylist and became close friends with Katniss. He is a Capitol citizen but does not dress as flamboyantly as the rest of the citizens. He is supportive, clever and designs stunning outfits for Katniss.
Seneca Crane
The head game maker of the hunger games
Caesar Flickerman
The tv host that interviews the tributes in the hunger games.
President Snow
The leader of Panem

District 12

Asterid Everdeen
Asterid is the mother of Katniss and Primrose who went through a great depression because of the death of his husband in a mining accident. As a result , Katniss was left to take care of her sister and feed her family.
Primrose Everdeen
She is the younger sister of Katniss. When chosen for the hunger games as a tribute , Katniss volunteers to take her place.Prim has a heart full of compassion and an affinity for healing.

Other Tributes

  • Rue (District 11) – A young, clever girl who forms an alliance with Katniss.
  • Thresh (District 11) – Strong and quiet; shows Katniss mercy in the arena.
  • Cato (District 2) – A strong, aggressive Career tribute.
  • Clove (District 2) – A skilled knife-thrower and Career tribute.
  • Glimmer (District 1) – A Career tribute, dies from tracker jacker stings.
  • Marvel (District 1) – A Career tribute; kills Rue.
  • Foxface (District 5) – Clever and sneaky; avoids direct conflict.
  • Other unnamed tributes – From Districts 3, 4, 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10.
